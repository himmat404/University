About

P P Savani Education Trust was launched in 1987, initially with a school, which over the years expanded itself with a group of schools in Surat district with a s tudent strength of more than 45,000.In 2016,the Trust has expanded its horizon with the launch of P.P.Savani University,as an initiative in higher education aligned with global standards of excellence. Through this immersive educational endeavour, the Governing body aims to introduce South Gujarat to a new era in higher education and create a talent pool of professionally sensitized industry-ready professionals. A world class 100 acre campus has been developed which embodies an infrastructure facilitating undergraduate postgraduate,research, certificate and skill-development programmes.
